Differential Pressure Gauge

Single Diaphram DPG

Differential pressure gauges with single diaphragm instrments are used to check differential pressures of liquids which do not have high viscosity and do not crystalize. It is available in dry & liquid versions he sensing element is diaphragm.

Measurement range

0 – 250 Pa, 0 – 500 Pa, 0 – 1 kPa, 0 – 10 kPa (custom ranges available)

Accuracy

±1.6% F.S. (standard), ±1% F.S. (high-accuracy)

Operating temperature

-20°C to +80°C (−4°F to +176°F)

Application areas

Filters, clean rooms, ventilation ducts, compressors, blowers, and pump monitoring

Max working pressure

Up to 700 kPa (100 psi), depending on model

Process connection

1/8" NPT, 1/4" NPT, or G1/8

Materials

Housing: Aluminum or Polycarbonate
Diaphragm: Silicone or NBR rubber
Window: Acrylic

Additional options

Front flange, rear flange, mounting brackets, electrical contacts
